About Me

Hi 👋 my name is Liam and I enjoy building things that live on the internet. My interest in software development began in 2015 when I began studying Computer Science and Math at Vassar College, and shortly thereafter began working as a Computer Science Department Coach/TA assisting my peers in their coursework. In the summer of 2018 before my final year at Vassar I attended FullStack Academy in New York City where I kick-started my career in web development.

Fast-forward to today, and I’ve had the privilege of working at a battery start-up, and a global developer platform. My main focus these days is building tools to accelerate the adoption and usage of GitHub actions at GitHub for a variety of enterprise clients.

Here are a few technologies I’ve been working with recently:

  • TypeScript
  • Rust
  • Svelte
  • C#
  • Ruby
  • Kubernetes
